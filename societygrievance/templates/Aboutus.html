Overview

Complaints are a valuable source of feedback to improve the infrastructure and condition of our city. The citizens may have complaints with respect to their environment and city`s infrastructure but they might not like the traditional complaining system in which they have to undergo a long procedure like going to the office and standing there for hours in queue, wasting so much of their valuable time and efforts. So that proposed system implements a new citizen facilitation process with compliant raising technique. This is an online application developed for the public citizen. This website gives the details about the details of every ward with their basic details such as councilor, president and MLA details. So every citizen can raise a compliant through online with their unique Aadhar number. The citizen can take photos regarding the complaints about the road facility, drainage, water and electricity problems. The complaints first will be informed to the councilor with the pictures along with some time limit. If the authorized person failed to respond within the specific period, the same compliant will be forwarded to the president. Again If the authorized person failed to respond within the specific period, the same compliant will be forwarded to the MLA. If the authorized person respond the particular complaint means this complaint response information send to citizens Mail Id.finally Higher Authority (CM) can view all the report regards complaint pending and complaint clear information. The proposed project facilitates the complete citizen facilitation process with the effective information gathering. The proposed complaint system improves the compliant processing easiest manner.
