Welcome to Society Grivence Facilitation Center Website

The website developed to make the new system extremely user friendly The proposed system implements a new citizen facilitation process with corporation details gathering and compliant raising technique. Proposed system User can log on the system and they can share take photos regarding the complaints about the road facility, drainage, and water and electricity problems. The complaints will be informed to the ward member and councilor with the pictures. The citizen can give priority about the complaint. The contributed fund details are enclosed in the website. So every citizen can view and know about their locality information. The proposed system has been designed to eliminate the major disadvantages of the existing system.
The proposed citizen complaint system improves the compliant processing system with active alert and conditional complaint forwarding process to the higher authority. The application provides fast and reliable system to send complaints and view funds and other details of their locality.

 

Features:

 large volumes of data can be stored with ease.
 Security is assured.
 Proposed system which provide easiest way of raising complaints
 every citizen can view and know about their locality information
 Maintenance of file is flexible.
 Records stored are updated now and then.
